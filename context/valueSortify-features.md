FEATURE_IDEAS:
## 1. Comparison Mode / History
No way to compare current sort results with a previous session. Add ability to save named snapshots and compare side-by-side.

## 2. Export as Shareable Image
Generate a visual summary card (canvas or SVG) of top 10 values that can be saved/shared. More engaging than CSV export.

## 3. Progress Persistence Indicator
No visual cue that progress is auto-saved to localStorage. A subtle "Progress saved" toast or indicator would reassure users mid-sort that they can safely close and return.
